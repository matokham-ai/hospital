## Dashboard Impact

When you switch between branches, you should see:

1. **Different patient counts** - HQ001 shows 25, BR002 shows 15, BR003 shows 8
2. **Different appointment volumes** - Clear variation in upcoming appointments
3. **Different bed occupancy rates** - HQ001 busiest, BR003 quietest
4. **Different revenue totals** - HQ001 generates most revenue
5. **Different outstanding balances** - Payment collection varies by branch
6. **Different pharmacy stock levels** - HQ001 best stocked
7. **Different encounter types** - HQ001 has more IPD and emergency cases

The data now reflects realistic scenarios where the main hospital is busier and more profitable, while satellite branches have lower volumes.
